Privacy Policy

Dara & Sara Muslim respects your privacy and is committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect (if any), how it is used, and your rights.

- The app works fully offline and does not request any permissions.
- We do not collect personal data such as names, emails, phone numbers, or location.
- No analytics, advertisements, or trackers are included.

2. How We Use Your Data

Since no personal information is collected, there is nothing stored or shared. Any settings or preferences remain on your device only.

3. Permissions

The app requests no device permissions.

4. Security

Because no personal information is collected, security risks are minimal. Any preferences stored remain private on your own Android device.

5. Children’s Privacy

This app is designed for children. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children. If a parent believes data was provided, please contact us and we will remove it immediately.
